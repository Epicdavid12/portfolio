Au Bon Cake

“Designer bags you can eat.” Ads, product photography treatments, and web design for a boutique kosher bakery. Emphasis on my 3D CAD modeling for physical cookie cutters and chocolate molds.

Graphic DesignAdvertisingPhotography3D PrintingLaser CuttingTinkercadCADGlowforge

The Work

Au Bon Cake sculpts hyper-realistic cakes (handbags, fruit, clocks) and needed visuals that sold the double-take. I produced their ad campaigns, product shots, and site pages, keeping the luxury-parody tone front and center.
