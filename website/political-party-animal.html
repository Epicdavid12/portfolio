Political Party Animal

Win the election by herding voters, court donors, and gerrymandering your way to the presidency in a satirical mobile game about running the worst best campaign ever.

Unity EnginePixel ArtAnimationGame designGame programming

The Project

Political Party Animal is a mobile roguelike Deckbuilder inspired by Balatro and UFO 50 where every citizen is a “little guy.” Try to win the state by exceeding the popularity score each voter will give you. But every controversial supporter will bring trouble. Exceed 4 trouble and you’ll get cancelled. Use the money donors give you to buy more guys to add to your deck, with special abilities, and win the election by any means necessary.

The Roster

Every archetype pulls the campaign in a different direction: voters bring popularity, donors bring money, extremists bring trouble. And others can add unique skills to increase your score.
